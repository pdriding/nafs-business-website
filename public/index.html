Fire Protection Installation and Services

Welcome to FADS we are a leading fire alarm installation company operating in and around London.

Whether you own residential premises, such as new build houses or flats, or you manage a commercial building, such as offices and retail outlets, high quality fire detection methods are imperative to ensure optimum safety for occupants.

Based in London, FADS are ideally located to provide fast and reliable fire alarm installation throughout the London area. The company has gained a reputation for quality fire alarm system installation from both its customers and suppliers. Our expert team of fire alarm engineers have a wealth of experience plus all new fire alarm systems are guaranteed and backed up by a manufacturers warranty.
